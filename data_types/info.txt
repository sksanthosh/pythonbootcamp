 float- floating(2.0, 0.5, 100.0)

 list- Ordered sequences of the data [data1,data2,data3]

 dict- The values are mapped with the keys {k1:v1,k2:v2,k3:v3}

 tuple - Ordered unmutable means unchangable once declared them (1,"Hi",200.3)

 sets- Unorderd collections of unique elements {"a","b"}

 Boolean - Logical value indicating True or False
